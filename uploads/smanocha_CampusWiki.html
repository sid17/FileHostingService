Summer Opportunities for first year IITK Junta

IIT Kanpur is one of the most reputed technical institute in India.It gives the students with ample opportunities to shape one's future in the way one desires.Since most people undergo through the same curriculum during the semesters, the summers provide one with sufficient opportunities to explore one's specific interests.I will like to throw light on some of the opportunities available during summers. Specifically for first year IIT Junta.

The institute has a lot of active clubs that enable us to pursue technical as well as cultural interests.Some of the major clubs involve Programming Club, Robotics Club, Dance Club, Dramatics Club, etc.Most of the technical clubs offer projects for first year students spread over a time span of 40 days.Students are allowed to pursue any project during this time and has it has produced some awesome results.Pipe Climbing Robot, Open Source Presentation Editor, Programming Club website, Rubik's cube solving Robot and Online Programming Judge are some of the products of 40 day long hard work.

Summers also provide an opportunity to pursue our passion in any non technical field.Students may chose to be a part of the Sports Summer Camp and pursue their favourite sport and excel in it.This also provides one with an opportunity to represent the institute in the Inter IIT Sports meet held every year.One may also pursue cultural activities as Dramatics, Dance, etc. Dramatics Club hold workshops for stage as well as street plays during summers.Dance Club also invite trained professionals to help students learn different dance forms as classical , hip-hop etc.

Apart from the campus opportunities students also have a chance to do company based internships.People having prior knowledge in the field of web development can get an intern in some of the Bangalore or some campus based startups.One may also go for company internships as Tata Motors , Bosch etc though such internships generally do not offer any stipend or require some kind of recommendation from some company professionals.

Some students also choose to spend their summers differently and stay back and work upon their specific interests say practise algorithms and solving problems on Spoj,etc.Even the Association of Computing Activities (ACA) conduct lectures for Advanced C++, algorithms and Android Development at a minimal fee.

Pursuing a research project under under some professor at IIT Kanpur or institutes as TIFR,ISI etc is an equally good way to spend the summers. However, some of them require some prior work during the semesters and contacting the professors and discuss one's research interest.

Apart from the usual curriculum, summers are the best option for one to realise one's potential and knowing one's interest. This is specifically helpful to stay focussed and explore one's interest in the upcoming years of stay in the campus.Summers is the most fruitful time for one to pursue what one wishes to do rather than being driven in herds and studying hard just to get good grades in the exams.One must therefore utilise this opportunity and make the best out of it
